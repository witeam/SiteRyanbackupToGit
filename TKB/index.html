About Us

The first of its kind
in the Philippines and South East Asia

The first of its kind in the Philippines and South East Asia, The Kitchen Bookstore is an independent online bookstore specializing in books, magazines, periodicals and other printed materials on culinary culture and lifestyle, food and wine.

We take immense pride in serving up an extensive inventory of tantalizing titles, from the new and hotly anticipated to the rare and elusive, many of which (we have to smugly state) are no longer in print. We also carry limited editions and a lucky few may be even able to make away with a signed copy or two.

Our thirst for food literature is unquenchable and with time we shall be expanding our selection, sourcing titles from all corners of the globe. We are more than willing to trade and purchase books from fellow collectors too, the sole proviso being that they are in indisputably fine form.

There is little point in promising good quality food literature if the articles themselves are in ill health. Being shamelessly serious and fastidious book collectors, we at The Kitchen Bookstore would only carry what we want stultifying our shelves, bereft of blemishes, creases and dents, what we prefer to call “Collector’s Condition”. This is something we stand by.

As icing on the cake, itlog na maalat for the tocino or tarka for the sambhar, if you’ would indulge us, we have something else to offer: the safe and secure delivery of your ordered goods straight to your doorstep.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders ship within 1-3 working days from Manila via Xend, and a tracking number will be emailed to you when your package ships. Packages usually arrive in 4-5 working days. If you need it your order on a specific date, please email before ordering.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept PayPal and direct credit card payments (Visa, Mastercard & American Express). All payments are processed by PayPal for your safety and security. We do not store your credit card information.

I live outside of the Philippines. Can I still place an order?

Yes! For international orders please write us at orders@thekitchenbookstore.com for more information.

What is your return policy?

Unfortunately, we do not accept returns.

What is your privacy policy?

Your information is solely used to process your order and will never be shared with other parties. You will never be contacted with promotions or announcements unless you opt-in to email updates.

I'd like a large quantity of an item; is this possible?

Bulk quantities are available for many items! Write us at orders@thekitchenbookstore.com to find out how much is in stock and to get a custom shipping quote.

Can I share your images?

You are more than welcome to use The Kitchen Bookstore product photos on your blog. If you do, please just link back to the thekitchenbookstore.com homepage or the specific product page. Photos may not be used for any other purpose and may not be altered. All content on this website is copyrighted. Should you require high-resolution images for features, write as at info@thekitchenbookstore.com.

Still have a question?

Write us at info@thekitchenbookstore.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
